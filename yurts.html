Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?

Your luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove.The luxury camping experience also includes electriciy and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guest also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
